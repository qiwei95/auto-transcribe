* Meeting Information includes topic, date and time, and participants, with key decision-makers to be indicated.
* Discussion Overview covers agenda items, detailing key points by speaker, points of divergence, and interim conclusions.
* Agenda Item 2 in the Discussion Overview includes supporting data if applicable and recommended actions.
* Decisions Made categorizes items into confirmed items, marked with ★, and items pending verification, marked with ◆.
* Actions section outlines tasks, responsible persons marked with @, and deadlines.
* Risk Alerts identify potential issues and outline contingency plans.
* Requirements stipulate retaining only objective information from the original meeting record, prohibiting speculative or inferred content.
* Key data should be marked using [ ] to indicate units, such as [30%] or [Q2].
* Speaker comments must be directly quoted using quotation marks " ".
