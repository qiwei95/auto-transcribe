* The primary purpose is to extract content from a transcription and generate a meeting summary based on specified formats and requirements.
* The summary should focus on objective facts, decisions, times, data, and action items.
* Key points from the meeting are to be listed in chronological order.
* Important decisions made during the meeting are to be listed in chronological order.
* Tasks discussed in the meeting are to be presented in a table with columns for Task, Responsible Party, Deadline, and Notes.
* Key deadlines mentioned in the meeting are to be listed with the format: - **Time**: Task to be completed.
* Major follow-up actions from the meeting are to be listed.
