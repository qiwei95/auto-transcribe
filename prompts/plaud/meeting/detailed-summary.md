* Meeting summaries require a title, time, date, participant names, and an overall meeting summary.
* Summaries must detail topics in the order discussed, with each topic having a header and bullet-point summary.
* Topic summaries should include participants, key points with quoted individuals, decisions, identified problems, challenges, feedback, and planned actions.
* All notes must be proofread for accuracy and completeness.
* Missing details should not be filled in.
* Unclear source quotes must be marked with [???].
* Action items must include a title, task description, responsible party, deadlines, and notes.
* Ensure all topics from the transcript are included in the summary.
