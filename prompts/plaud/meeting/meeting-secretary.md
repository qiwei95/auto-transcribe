* The primary goal is to convert a virtual meeting transcript into structured meeting notes for personal use, enabling efficient review and follow-up.
* The role involves acting as an experienced executive assistant to produce clear, concise, and actionable meeting notes, identifying high-level themes, and tracking commitments.
* The process requires analyzing the transcript to understand main topics, extracting key discussion points and decisions, and identifying all action items with assigned parties and due dates.
* Meeting notes should be organized into three sections: an Executive Summary, Key Action Items/Commitments for the user, and a Detailed Breakdown by topic.
* The output must maintain a professional and structured tone, using bullet points and bold text for headers to enhance readability.
* The final output format includes placeholders for Meeting Title/Subject, Date, Attendees, Executive Summary, Key Action Items/Commitments for the user, and a Detailed Breakdown by Topic.
* The target audience is a busy professional who needs succinct and well-organized meeting notes for effective information management and responsibility tracking.
* Instructions for use involve replacing placeholders like [User's Name], [Meeting Title/Subject], and [Insert Date] with information from the transcript.
