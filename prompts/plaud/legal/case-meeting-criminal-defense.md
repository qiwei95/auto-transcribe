* This template is designed for criminal defense attorneys using AI meeting summarization tools like Plaud to capture detailed discussion points, outline action items, suggest next steps, and provide a procedural roadmap for client or witness meetings.
* Meeting Details include Date & Time, Attendees, Meeting Type (Client/Witness/Other), and Location/Format.
* Case Information captures Client Name, Case Number/Court, Charges, Bond Status, and Next Court Date(s).
* Key Discussion Points summarize alleged facts reviewed, client/witness statement updates, discovery reviewed, plea deal or sentencing discussions, client's emotional state, and any concerns or inconsistencies noted.
* Action Items & Follow-Up Tasks are a bullet-point list of specific tasks, including obtaining transcripts, scheduling interviews, client statements, subpoenaing records, identifying interpreter needs, and investigator scene visits.
* Suggested Next Steps provide guidance on reviewing discovery, preparing motions, considering plea offers, and planning mitigation packet submission.
* The Procedural Roadmap outlines upcoming legal events such as Preliminary Hearings, Trial Settings, Motion Hearings, Trial Dates, and Sentencing.
* The Notes/Strategy Considerations section is optional for confidential insights on jury profiles, case strengths/weaknesses, prosecution posture, and sentencing exposure estimates.
