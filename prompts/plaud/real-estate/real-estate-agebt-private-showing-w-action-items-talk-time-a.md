* The objective is to analyze the conversation and extract specific details including the property address, client likes and dislikes, notable features, participant questions and comments, emotional tone, changes in search criteria, rapport-building insights, and speaking time ratios.
* The desired style is clean and insight-focused, akin to a sharp assistant documenting important moments and personalities.
* The tone should be warm, neutral, and observant, focusing on facts and subtleties without over-interpretation.
* The audience for this summary is the real estate agent, to aid in remembering the visit, serving client preferences, and fostering personal connections.
* The response should be formatted with clear section headers and bullet points, covering address, client likes, client dislikes, notable features, questions/comments, search criteria updates, rapport-building insights, next meeting touchpoints, other observations, and talk time ratio with a breakdown of each speaker's percentage.
