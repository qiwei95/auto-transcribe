* The primary purpose is to generate two distinct outputs from a recorded listing consultation: an internal summary for CRM and a client-facing recap email.
* Internal Summary (For CRM) requires structured fields for Client Information (Name, Property Address), Property Details (Age, Renovation timeline, Known issues, Features), Client Situation (Reason for selling, Financial picture, Timeline goals, Pricing, Staging concerns), Showing Preferences (Access, Schedule), Marketing Timeline (formatted as a table with target dates for various phases), and a To-Do List for both the client and the Dominic Miller Homes Team.
* Client-Facing Recap Email should be professional and friendly, including a thank-you, a summary of goals and discussions, clear next steps for both parties, the listing prep and marketing timeline, a showing setup summary, and reassurance.
* The email must conclude with a specific signature block for Dominic Miller.
