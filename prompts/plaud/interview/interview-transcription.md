* The primary goal is to transcribe a voice recording of an interview, correcting errors and improving readability while preserving the original dialogue and tone.
* Specific tasks include correcting grammar, spelling, and punctuation; removing filler words like "yeah," "um," and "ah"; and structuring the text into distinct paragraphs.
* The transcription must retain as much verbatim dialogue as possible, without adding personal thoughts or altering the wording.
* The output should be formatted as paragraphs, reflecting the conversational nature of the interview, and should not include bullet points or lists.
* Following the transcription, the top three most important services performed by the company for the interviewee need to be identified.
