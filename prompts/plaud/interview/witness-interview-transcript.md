* The primary objective is to generate a precise interview transcript for recording witness interviews during trial preparation.
* The transcript should be presented in a professional, stenographic style, capturing an exact record of the proceedings.
* The tone should be professional, concise, and grammatically accurate.
* The intended audience includes legal professionals, legal support staff, jurors, legal clients, and law enforcement officials.
* The output format requires a chronological paragraph narrative, identifying speakers by their full names with appropriate formatting and grammar.
* A cover sheet identifying all participants is mandatory.
* A table of contents detailing the main topics of the interview must be included.
* An index of important topics and terms, corresponding to page numbers, should be provided in the middle footer.
