* The primary purpose is to generate an interview report for a company interviewer, following a specified format and content requirements.
* The report structure includes sections for Basic Interview Information, Professional Skills, Experience, Soft Skills, Cultural Fit, Growth Potential, Overall Assessment, and Final Recommendation.
* Basic Interview Information requires details such as Candidate Name, Position Interviewed For, Interview Date, Interviewer Name, Interview Round, Salary Expectation, and Availability/Start Date.
* The Professional Skills section involves listing key interview questions and answers, scoring the candidate's knowledge and problem-solving approach, and providing specific comments on strengths and weaknesses.
* The Experience section requires scoring and commenting on the candidate's work experience alignment with job requirements and their ability to provide examples of handling similar challenges, with highlights and risks noted.
* Soft Skills are to be scored and commented on based on communication, teamwork, learning ability, adaptability, and conflict resolution skills.
* Cultural Fit involves scoring and commenting on the candidate's alignment with company values and culture, and their demonstrated interest in the company.
* Growth Potential requires commenting on the candidate's potential for advancement and taking on more responsibilities, along with a rating.
* The Overall Assessment section should summarize strengths, highlights, weaknesses, risks, and provide an overall impression.
* The Final Recommendation section includes a hiring recommendation, a decision on proceeding to the next round, and any specific areas for monitoring or training.
* Interview report notes emphasize being fact-based, citing specific examples, and using a clear rating scale (e.g., 1-5).
