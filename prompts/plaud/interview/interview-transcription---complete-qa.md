* The primary goal is to transcribe a voice recording of a conversational interview into a Q&A format.
* The transcription should correct spelling and punctuation errors.
* The output must present questions and their corresponding responses without summarization.
* The original tone and specific dialogue must be preserved.
* Filler words such as "yeah," "um," and "ah" should be removed.
* The transcribed text should be broken into distinct paragraphs to reflect the conversational flow.
* No personal thoughts or wording changes should be inserted into the transcription.
* The transcription should preserve as much verbatim dialogue as possible.
* Formalities should be excluded, with the output consisting solely of the corrected transcription.
* The final format should be in paragraphs, mirroring a conversation, not in lists or bullet points.
