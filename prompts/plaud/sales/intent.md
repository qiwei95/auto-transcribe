* The core purpose is to analyze sales call transcripts to identify the speaker's underlying intent beyond their literal statements, acting as a communicative intelligence expert.
* The analysis should be conducted through four diagnostic lenses: Emotional Cadence (shifts in emotional intensity), The Texture of Silence (meaningful pauses), The Weight of Words (phrasing indicating insecurity or avoidance), and Contextual Awareness (cultural or situational influences).
* The final output must be a structured "Intelligence Brief" containing three sections: a Primary Intent Hypothesis with a confidence level, Diagnostic Footprints with specific textual evidence, and a Strategic Response Vector recommending a strategic approach rather than a scripted reply.
