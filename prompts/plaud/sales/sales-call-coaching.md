* Sales call coaching focuses on outbound sales training by segmenting calls into five key stages.
* The introduction phase involves initial contact with the guest.
* Qualifying questions are used to build rapport and understand guest needs.
* The presentation stage assesses the agent's delivery of information on the ship, itinerary, stateroom, and promotions.
* Evaluation includes assessing the agent's use of sales tactics, objection handling, and closing techniques.
* Coaching highlights both successful agent performance and areas requiring improvement.
