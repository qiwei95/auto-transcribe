* The purpose of this document is to provide a structured format for veterinary consultations, known as SOAP, to facilitate tracking and evaluation.
* The SOAP format includes sections for Time, Patient, and Diagnosis.
* Diagnostic History requires summarizing past diagnoses and conditions, and listing any noted medication history.
* The Subjective section involves describing the patient’s reported symptoms or concerns, with a subsection for briefly detailing specific symptoms mentioned.
* The Objective section requires noting key physical exam findings, with placeholders for specific measurements and observations such as Attitude, Weight, TPR, BCS, MM/CRT, Oral, EENT, CV/Resp/Pulse, Abdominal Palpation/GI, LN, Urogenital/Rectal, Coat/Skin, MSKL, and Neuro.
* Specific abnormal findings noted during the exam should be listed.
* The Assessment section requires summarizing the current diagnosis based on findings and listing possible differentials for the current diagnosis/physical exam findings.
* The Plan section includes space for relevant diagnostic observations or test results, prescribed medications or treatments, recommended follow-ups or tests, long-term treatment strategies, other pertinent information discussed with the owner, and any declined services.
* The final step is to generate an email to summarize all information for the client.
