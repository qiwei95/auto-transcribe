* Meeting Agenda & Objectives: Outline topics and client's primary goals for the meeting.
* Client Updates: Document life changes such as family, job, or health, and any new financial priorities or concerns.
* Financial Overview: Review income, expenses, assets, liabilities, and recent financial statements.
* Discussion Points: Address short- and long-term goals, investment strategy, risk tolerance, and planning for tax, insurance, estate, or retirement.
* Action Items: List tasks for both the advisor and the client, including deadlines and follow-up plans.
* Next Steps & Follow-Up: Schedule the next meeting and identify necessary documents or information.
* Advisor Notes & Observations: Record key insights, concerns, and recommendations.
