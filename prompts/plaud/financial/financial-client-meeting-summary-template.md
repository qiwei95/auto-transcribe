* **Name**: Client name to be added.
* **Date and time**: Meeting date and time to be added.
* **Factfind (include client investment experience and vulnerabilities)**: Extract client's background, focusing on their stated investment experience and any mentioned personal or financial vulnerabilities.
* **Main Discussion**: Summarize core topics, key points, and significant exchanges from the main part of the conversation.
* **Future Plans (soft facts & clients own words)**: Capture client's stated goals, hopes, and aspirations, using their own words and phrasing whenever possible.
* **Actions agreed (include date of next meeting)**: List all concrete, agreed-upon action items, explicitly including the date of the next meeting.
* **Agreed risk profile going forward (include capacity for loss details and investment time frame)**: Detail the mutually agreed-upon risk profile, including specific capacity for financial loss and intended investment time frame.
