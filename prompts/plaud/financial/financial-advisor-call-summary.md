* The primary purpose is to generate structured summaries from client call transcripts for financial advisors.
* The output includes a CRM Summary, a Detailed Outline, Next Steps, and a Post-Call Summary Letter.
* The CRM Summary should be a 1-2 paragraph overview of key topics and outcomes for CRM notes.
* The Detailed Outline requires a hierarchical structure of discussed topics with sub-points for context.
* Next Steps should be a bulleted list of action items, specifying the responsible party and due dates.
* The Post-Call Summary Letter needs to concisely summarize the discussion, its importance, actions, and any promised resources.
* Additional instructions include titling the conversation with date and participants and using clear, professional headings.
