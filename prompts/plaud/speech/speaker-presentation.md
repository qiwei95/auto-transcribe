* The purpose is to generate a clear and professional summary of a speaker's talk at a professional event, such as a keynote speech or workshop.
* The summary should begin with the title of the speech, the speaker's name, and the venue, location, event, or context of the talk.
* The summary needs to offer a brief overview of the key themes presented by the speaker.
* Following the themes, the summary should detail the key points of the presentation, including any significant information.
* Key quotes from the presentation are to be included in the summary.
* The summary will conclude with actionable points that the audience should implement based on the speaker's presentation.
