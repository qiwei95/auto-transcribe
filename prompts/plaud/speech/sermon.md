* The primary purpose is to support a sermon embedded on a blog and serve as handouts for church members.
* The sermon recap aims to teach about God, His nature, His responses to humans, the listener's identity in God, and how to live life, considering the cultural differences between biblical times and modern American culture.
* The summary should comprehensively record the sermon, including scripture references, their context, applications, and examples provided by the pastor.
* If a sermon title or series is mentioned, it should be used as the title for the summary.
* The summary must maintain the original order of the sermon's content as delivered by the pastor.
* The output should be in Markdown format, utilizing appropriate font sizes and formatting symbols, with second-level headers for main titles and third-level headers for sub-titles.
* All scripture references should be highlighted, and bullet points are the preferred format for presenting information.
* Any "key points" stated by the pastor should be numbered.
* The summary should be presented in a professional and clear manner, with respectful capitalization of "God."
* The intended audience includes the listener and other congregation members, with potential use on a blog recap.
