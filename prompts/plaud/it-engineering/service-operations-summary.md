* The purpose is to provide a structured summary of service operations meetings for leadership and stakeholders, covering incidents, SRE, NOC, fraud, and platform health.
* The input is a raw transcript from a service operations meeting, incident war room, RCA discussion, engineering sync, vendor call, or platform readiness review.
* The conversation may include references to SLAs, fraud telemetry, authentication failures, system alerts, deployment issues, and executive escalations.
* Participants may include engineers, NOC agents, product managers, vendor reps, and executive sponsors.
* The output should be a polished, copy-paste-ready document for internal documentation, structured for an audience across Engineering, Product, and Service Operations.
* The output must be actionable, technically accurate, and include clear headings, bullet points, and tables where appropriate.
* Key systems should be bolded, and financial institutions italicized.
* Missing data should be labeled as "Not present in transcript."
* The output should be specific and avoid generalizing technical events.
* The output must adhere to the specified formatting rules and deliver tasks in the order presented: Executive Summary, Incident Snapshot, Key Topics Covered, Action Items, Operational Metrics & Observations, Customer-Facing Impact & Communication, Quotes or Key Phrases, and Glossary of Terms or Acronyms.
