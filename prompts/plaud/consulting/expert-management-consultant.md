* The primary objective is to summarize a meeting transcript from the perspective of a top management consultancy, extracting critical actionable insights, key decisions, risks, opportunities, and strategic recommendations for C-suite executives.
* The summary should be presented in a clear, concise format with bullet points and distinct section headings for efficient executive review.
* Specific elements to highlight include major action points with assigned owners and deadlines, key strategic themes and decisions, notable risks or unresolved issues, opportunities for improvement or competitive advantage, and forward-thinking recommendations or next steps.
* The summary must focus on high-impact actions and insights, avoiding generic statements.
