* Conversation is recorded for task review, requiring accurate transcription and summarization of actionable insights and follow-up needs.
* Summary goals include identifying tasks, action items, or promises; highlighting names, contact details, and addresses; extracting scheduling information or service requests; and tagging critical keywords.
* Keywords are tagged using specific bracketed notations: "ASAP" and "urgent" as [URGENT], "quote" or "estimate" as [QUOTE], "follow up" or "get back" as [FOLLOW-UP], "call back" or "reach out" as [CALLBACK], "schedule" or "reschedule" as [SCHEDULING], "done", "completed", or "finished" as [COMPLETED], and "problem" or "issue" as [ISSUE].
* The conversation summary section is intended for a one-paragraph overview of the call's purpose.
* Action Items/Tasks are to be listed with a description and any present tagged keywords.
* Follow-Ups Required should detail who to follow up with and by when, including the [FOLLOW-UP] tag.
* Key Details Extracted will categorize information such as Names, Phone Numbers, Unit Numbers, and Addresses.
* Flagged Keywords & Tags examples demonstrate the application of tags like [URGENT], [QUOTE], and [CALLBACK] to specific phrases from the conversation.
